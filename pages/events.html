Events

Axe Throwing Night


Step into the thrill zone and test your aim with an evening of exciting axe throwing! Whether you’re a first-timer or a seasoned thrower, our trained staff will guide you through safety, techniques, and friendly competition. Perfect for groups, date nights, or team-building, this high-energy activity promises plenty of laughs, bragging rights, and unforgettable moments.

Trivia


Test your knowledge, flex your brain, and enjoy some friendly competition! Join us for a night filled with challenging questions, laughter, and great company. Whether you’re a seasoned trivia champ or just here for the fun, you’ll have a blast competing for prizes and bragging rights. Grab your friends, form your team, and see if you have what it takes to be the ultimate trivia master!

Open Dance Floor


Hit the floor and dance the night away! From timeless classics to party favorites, the music keeps the energy high and the celebration going strong. You can request your favorite songs, or you can let our in house DJ choose what jams you boogie down to. If you need shoes, these will be provded for you and your guests free of charge.

Wine Tasting


Sip, swirl, and savor an evening of flavor! Join us for a guided tasting featuring a curated selection of wines from near and far. Whether you’re a seasoned wine enthusiast or just beginning your journey, you’ll enjoy exploring unique aromas, discovering new favorites, and learning a few tips along the way. Pair it all with great conversation and a relaxed atmosphere for the perfect night out.

Cocktail Hour

Reception Dinner


Savor a delicious meal while toasting to the happy couple. From heartfelt speeches to shared laughter, every bite and every moment is part of the love-filled celebration. We will serve you and your guests from our amazing food menu for a meal that you will remember for the rest of your life.
