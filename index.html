About Us



Here at Honey Bee, love is at the heart of everything we do—both in the celebrations we host and in the way we run our business.
As a husband-and-wife team, we understand the joy, emotion, and attention to detail that make weddings and intimate gatherings truly unforgettable. What started as a shared dream has grown into a warm, welcoming space where families and friends come together to create memories that last a lifetime. From your first tour to the last toast of the evening, we’re by your side—offering personal guidance, heartfelt hospitality, and the reassurance that every detail is in caring hands. Whether you’re envisioning a romantic wedding, a milestone celebration, or a cozy private event, our mission is to make it uniquely yours. Here, every guest is treated like family, every love story is celebrated, and every moment matters.
